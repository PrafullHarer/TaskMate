## üë• Managing Groups

Groups are the core of Accountable. You can belong to multiple groups (e.g., "Study Buddies", "Gym Crew", "Coding Bootcamp").

### Creating a Group
1. Go to the **Groups** tab in the sidebar.
2. Click **"Create Group"**.
3. Give your group a unique **Name** and **Description**.
4. You are now the **Admin** of this group!

### Joining a Group
1. Ask a group member for their **Invite Code**.
2. Go to **Groups** > **"Join Group"**.
3. Enter the code to instantly become a member.

### Group Settings (Admins Only)
As an Admin, you can access the **Settings** tab within your group page to:
- **Rename** the group.
- **Remove Members**: Manage who is in your circle.
- **Configure Reset Frequency**: Choose how often the leaderboard resets (Weekly, Bi-weekly, or Monthly).

## ‚úÖ Task Management

Hold yourself accountable by tracking your daily goals.

### Creating a Task
1. Inside a group, click **"New Task"** (or use the helper on the dashboard).
2. Fill in the details:
   - **Title**: What do you need to do?
   - **Video/Description**: Optional details.
   - **Priority**: Low, Medium, or High (Higher priority = More coins!).
   - **Effort**: Scale of 1-5.
   - **Due Date**: When must this be done?
3. Click **Create**.

### Completing Tasks
- When you finish a task, find it in your list and click **"Complete"**.
- üéâ You will **instantly** earn **Coins** based on the task's difficulty. The coin balance in the header updates immediately!

### ‚ö†Ô∏è Overdue Tasks & Penalties
- **Be careful!** If a task passes its Due Date without being completed, it becomes **Overdue**.
- Our system checks for overdue tasks **hourly**.
- You will be **penalized 20 Coins** for each overdue task. Don't let your score drop!

## üí∞ How Coins are Calculated

Every task earns you coins based on a smart formula designed to reward impact and hard work:

`Base Score (10) √ó Priority Multiplier √ó Effort Multiplier`

### Multipliers Breakdown

| Priority | Multiplier | | Effort Level | Multiplier |
|----------|------------|-|--------------|------------|
| **Low**      | 1x         | | **1** (Quick)     | 0.5x       |
| **Medium**   | 1.5x       | | **2**             | 1.0x       |
| **High**     | 2x         | | **3** (Moderate)  | 1.5x       |
|              |            | | **4**             | 2.0x       |
|              |            | | **5** (Intense)   | 2.5x       |

### Examples
- **Easy Chore**: Low Priority + Effort 1 = `10 √ó 1.0 √ó 0.5` = **5 Coins**
- **Standard Work**: Medium Priority + Effort 3 = `10 √ó 1.5 √ó 1.5` = **23 Coins**
- **Major Milestone**: High Priority + Effort 5 = `10 √ó 2.0 √ó 2.5` = **50 Coins**

### ‚õî Negative Coin Rules
Accountability cuts both ways! If you fail to complete your tasks on time:
- **Flat Penalty**: You lose **20 Coins** for every overdue task.
- **Negative Balance**: Yes, your coin balance **can go below zero** (e.g., -20 Coins).
- **Recovery**: The only way to get out of the "red" is to complete new tasks!

## ü™ô The Economy: Coins vs. Points

We use two types of currency to track your progress:

### 1. Coins (Group-Specific)
- **What are they?** Currency you earn within a specific group.
- **Usage**: Determines your rank on that group's Weekly Leaderboard.
- **Reset**: Your coin balance resets to 0 periodically (based on Group Settings), giving everyone a fresh start to win the "Cycle".

### 2. Global Points (Lifetime)
- **What are they?** A measure of your total lifetime achievement across **ALL** groups.
- **Conversion**: **10 Coins = 1 Global Point**.
- **Usage**: Determines your rank on the **Global Leaderboard**.
- **Reset**: Never! These points accumulate forever as a badge of honor.
